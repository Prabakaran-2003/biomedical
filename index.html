About

I am Prabakaran S, a Biomedical Engineering graduate from Kongunadu College of Engineering and Technology. Passionate about blending engineering and healthcare, I’ve worked on projects like a Heart Attack Detection System, IoT-based Paralysis Care, and a Wearable Bio-Monitoring T-shirt. My hands-on experience through hospital internships and certifications in Arduino, biomedical hardware, and medical coding has strengthened my technical expertise. I am a quick learner, dedicated team player, and effective communicator. My goal is to contribute to innovative healthcare solutions that enhance patient care and medical efficiency through technology-driven research and development.
